About Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online video game developed by Epic Games. It is available in three distinct game mode versions that share the same general gameplay and game engine: Fortnite: Save the World, Fortnite Battle Royale, and Fortnite Creative.

Fortnite Battle Royale

Fortnite Battle Royale is the most popular mode, where up to 100 players battle it out on an island to be the last person standing. The game starts with players parachuting onto the island and scavenging for weapons, resources, and other items to help them survive.

Fortnite Creative

Fortnite Creative is a mode where players can build their own islands and games using the in-game creation tools.
