Gameplay

Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical first-person shooter game where players take control of various unique operators from different countries. The main objective is to eliminate the opposing team or complete a specific objective, such as rescuing hostages or defending a site.

Characters

Each operator possesses their own unique abilities and gadgets, adding depth to the game's strategy. Players can choose operators from different factions like the United States, Russia, or the GROM.

Maps

Rainbow Six Siege features a variety of maps, each with its own distinct layout and strategic points. Maps are often inspired by real-world locations, adding to the game's immersive atmosphere.
